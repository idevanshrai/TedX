About TEDx

Bringing together innovators, thinkers, and doers to share ideas that matter

TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TED Talks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection.

Our event brings together leading thinkers and doers to share ideas that matter in a wide range of fields â€” science, business, the arts, global issues, and more.

Join us for a day of inspiring talks, networking opportunities, and thought-provoking discussions that will challenge your perspective and ignite new ideas.
